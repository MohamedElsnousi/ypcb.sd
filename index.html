who we are

Youth for Peace and Capacity Building Organization is a nonprofit Sudanese organization committed to making a real difference in the lives of communities across Sudan. We work on the ground, delivering essential services and creating sustainable programs that address the most pressing needs of displaced families and vulnerable populations. From establishing computer labs in technical colleges to distributing winter clothing and blankets to displaced families, our projects are designed to provide both immediate relief and long-term capacity building. We believe in transparency, accountability, and the power of collective action.
